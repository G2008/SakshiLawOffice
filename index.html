Specialized Legal Expertise

NDPS (Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances) Cases — Foreigners and NRIs

At Sakshi Law Office, we possess extensive experience and a proven track record in defending individuals, particularly foreign nationals and NRIs, charged under the stringent provisions of the NDPS Act in India.

NDPS cases require meticulous scrutiny of procedural compliance, including search, seizure, arrest, and forensic reports. Our approach focuses on:

  • Challenging procedural lapses and illegal detentions.
  • Securing bail for foreigners and NRIs with a strategic understanding of flight-risk concerns.
  • Minimizing travel requirements for overseas clients through robust representation.
  • Handling cases involving commercial quantity and international trafficking charges with tailored defense strategies.
  • Coordinating with embassies, consulates, and family members for smooth case management.

We have successfully defended clients in Delhi, Mumbai, Goa, Haryana, Punjab, Chandigarh, Kerela, Rajasthan, Uttar Pradesh and Bihar and major airports across India, ensuring that their rights are protected despite the harshness of the law. Our team ensures constant communication, careful documentation, and sensitive handling of international cases, recognizing the additional complexities involved.

Our commitment is to protect the dignity, freedom, and rights of every client, ensuring that the law is applied fairly and without prejudice.

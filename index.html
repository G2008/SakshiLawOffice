About Advocate Sakshi Kaul

Advocate Sakshi Kaul is a dedicated legal professional committed to delivering justice with integrity and diligence. She completed her law degree in 2012 and has been practicing in various legal fields, including Criminal Law, Family Law, Consumer Protection, and Motor Accident Claims.

Enrolled with the Bar Council of Delhi in 2012, she is also a proud member of the All India Bar Association. With years of experience, she has built a reputation for her strong legal acumen, client-focused approach, and strategic representation in complex legal matters.
